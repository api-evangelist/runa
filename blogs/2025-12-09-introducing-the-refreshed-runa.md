<p>Somewhere along the way, fintech brands have started to look and sound the same. A parking lot of Toyota Camrys—reliable, sure, but forgettable. Logos in the same colors. Language that blurs together. Promises that sound interchangeable. It’s a landscape optimized for safety, not distinction.</p>
<p><span id="more-27065"></span></p>
<p>Runa has never been that kind of company, and the work our customers do with us deserves more than a generic fintech uniform. Our customers create recognition programs, incentive moments, and global payout experiences that carry emotional weight. Their impact is human, not transactional.</p>
<p>Our refreshed brand reflects that reality. It is more direct, more modern, and more aligned with what it actually feels like to use Runa: intuitive infrastructure that removes friction and amplifies connection.<br />
This is not a cosmetic update. It is a clearer expression of who we have become.</p>
<h3><strong><img alt="runa-social" height="1079" src="https://runa.io/hs-fs/hubfs/runa-social.webp?width=1810&amp;height=1079&amp;name=runa-social.webp" style="height: auto; width: 1810px;" width="1810" /></strong></h3>
<h3><strong>Why Now?</strong></h3>
<p>Our platform has evolved from gift and prepaid cards in a few dozen countries to new payout types like push to debit across 190 countries.</p>
<p>The companies building on us have evolved, too. Many started with reward or recognition use cases. Today, they are orchestrating global payout programs that reinforce culture, deepen loyalty, and help people feel valued at scale. What has changed is the breadth of what we can help our customers achieve, and we wanted to make sure our brand captured that sentiment.</p>
<p><img alt="runa-swag" height="1080" src="https://runa.io/hs-fs/hubfs/runa-swag.webp?width=1810&amp;height=1080&amp;name=runa-swag.webp" style="height: auto; width: 1810px;" width="1810" /></p>
<h3><strong>It&#8217;s Not Just Payments Infrastructure. It&#8217;s Human Infrastructure.</strong></h3>
<p>“Go Further” became the organizing idea for the refresh because it captured something essential about Runa’s role.</p>
<p>When companies use Runa, they are not just sending money. They create moments that motivate, celebrate, and bring people closer together.  Recipients feel seen, and companies can reinforce their values through their payouts.</p>
<p>Internally, “Go Further” mirrors how we operate. It is a commitment to problem-solving with urgency, learning fast, and showing up for customers as partners, not vendors. The belief behind the line is what drives our teams to build infrastructure that feels simple even when the underlying complexity is anything but simple.</p>
<p><img alt="runa-deck" height="1080" src="https://runa.io/hs-fs/hubfs/runa-deck.webp?width=1810&amp;height=1080&amp;name=runa-deck.webp" style="height: auto; width: 1810px;" width="1810" /></p>
<h3><strong>The Road Ahead</strong></h3>
<p>And to our customers, especially those who have been with us since the beginning, this refresh is for you. It is a signal of our continued commitment to help you create more value, more connection, and more growth in the years ahead.</p>
<p>Runa has always stood for making money move as seamlessly as the internet itself. Now our brand moves with that same velocity and purpose.</p>
<p>We’re just getting started.</p>
