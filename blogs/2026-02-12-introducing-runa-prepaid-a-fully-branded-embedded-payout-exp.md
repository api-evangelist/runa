<p>Businesses today need payout solutions that are as flexible and customizable as the people they serve. Sometimes that means sending a gift card for instant choice, sometimes it’s a direct-to-bank transfer, and sometimes the right fit is a prepaid card.</p>
<p>With Runa, you don’t have to choose just one. From gift cards to push to card, to prepaid cards, every option is available through one seamless integration &#8211; giving you and your recipients the freedom to pick what works best, every time.</p>
<p>Now, we’re expanding that flexibility with the launch of Runa Prepaid Cards, a fully white-labeled, embedded payout option designed to fit seamlessly into your brand experience.</p>
<p><span id="more-27300"></span></p>
<h3>A Custom-Branded Card &amp; Embedded Experience</h3>
<p>Runa Prepaid Cards don’t just deliver funds &#8211; they deliver on experience. Businesses can customize every part of the journey:</p>
<ul>
<li>Custom card art to reflect your brand.</li>
<li>Branded recipient experience, including logo, colors, and messaging.</li>
<li>Embedded payout links that keep users in your experience &#8211; not redirected elsewhere.</li>
<li>It’s a truly white-label solution built for companies that want their brand to stay front and center.</li>
</ul>
<h3>Two Options. Endless Possibilities</h3>
<ol>
<li>Runa Reward Prepaid (Single-Use): For one-time moments that matter. A prepaid card with a fixed balance, ideal for rewards, rebates, promotions, or any single disbursement. Simple to send, easy to use, and accepted anywhere Visa works.</li>
<li>Runa Reload Prepaid (Reloadable): For ongoing payouts, made effortless. A reloadable prepaid card designed for recurring payments like disbursements, stipends or benefits. Recipients keep the same virtual card for future funds, no additional registration needed.</li>
</ol>
<p>Both options are delivered in seconds via secure URL, compatible with digital wallets and ready to scale globally.</p>
<h3>Why Prepaid, Why Now?</h3>
<p>The payout landscape is evolving, and expectations are higher than ever:</p>
<ul>
<li>92% of <a href="https://www.paymentsdive.com/press-release/20230718-gift-card-trends-consumer-preferences-behaviors" rel="noopener" target="_blank">recipients want choice</a> in where they spend.</li>
<li>67% of <a href="https://www.paymentsinnovationforum.org/articles/consumers-turn-to-gift-cards-to-help-mitigate-the-cost-of-living-rise" rel="noopener" target="_blank">recipients want access to multiple merchants</a>.</li>
</ul>
<p>Meanwhile, businesses need to scale payouts globally, without adding operational complexity. Runa Prepaid Cards meet those needs head-on. They offer a fast, flexible, and fully customizable payout option.</p>
<h3>One Platform. Unified Power</h3>
<p>Runa is more than just prepaid cards. It’s a full-suite payout platform built to support every scenario, whether you’re rewarding, reimbursing or scaling globally.</p>
<p>Through one API or portal, you get access to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Prepaid Cards: Single-use or reloadable Visa-powered flexibility.</li>

</ul>
