<p>The role of gift cards is changing fast. No longer just a seasonal sales boost or consumer afterthought, gift cards are becoming one of the most flexible, scalable tools in modern payouts, rewards and incentives.</p>

These aren’t just sales, they’re strategic placements that put your gift card in front of <em>high-intent</em> business audiences who buy at scale and spend with purpose.</p>
<p>At Runa, in 2025 alone we executed over 400 B2B promotional campaigns across 9 countries, driving results for more than 50 unique merchants. These campaigns have engaged 65+ enterprise customers, from global fintechs to innovative reward platforms.</p>
<p>And the results speak for themselves:</p>
<ul>
<li>21x average ROI delivered back to merchants</li>
<li>75% average lift in redemption compared to baseline activity</li>
<li>Long-tail value from first-time redemptions, reactivation, and repeat engagement</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>What B2B promotions unlock:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Featured placement in seasonal or always-on campaigns</li>
<li>Direct exposure to reward buyers and incentive managers</li>
<li>Measurable performance data tied to redemption, engagement, and reactivation</li>
</ul>

<h3>2. Recipient Choice Is a Multiplier &#8211;&nbsp;Make Sure You’re in the Mix</h3>
<p>When it comes to gift cards for rewards, incentives and payouts <em>choice matters.</em> <a href="https://capitaloneshopping.com/research/gift-card-statistics/?utm_source=chatgpt.com"><span>Data shows</span></a> that people are more likely to use and spend <em>above card value</em> when they can choose the brand or experience they want.</p>
<ul>
<li>Choice elevates perceived value.</li>
<li>Recipients who select their own card are more likely to redeem and overspend, increasing total transaction value and loyalty.</li>
<li>It modernizes the gifting experience beyond a single merchant interaction.</li>
</ul>

<li><a href="https://google.com/url?q=https://capitaloneshopping.com/research/digital-wallet-statistics?utm_source%3Dchatgpt.com&amp;sa=D&amp;source=docs&amp;ust=1767099407813028&amp;usg=AOvVaw0IfcHSshEbTvg8ZBjcZTqc" style="font-weight: normal;">Wallets already dominate</a><span style="font-weight: normal;"> in both online and in-store purchases</span></li>
<li><a href="https://www.giftcardpartners.com/blog/mobile-gift-card-trends-statistics#:~:text=In%202025%2C%20the%20global%20mobile,integrate%20them%20into%20loyalty%20initiatives." style="font-weight: normal;">70% of millennials</a><span style="font-weight: normal;"> prefer digital gift cards over physical ones&nbsp;and that number is rising</span></li>

<h3>4. Security Is Non-Negotiable &#8211; It&#8217;s What Keeps Value Where It Belongs</h3>
<p>Gift cards aren’t just popular &#8211;&nbsp;they’re powerful. But with that power comes risk. As gift cards become embedded into more customer journeys such as rewards, rebates and payouts, they’re also becoming a bigger target.</p>
<p>And fraud isn’t a theoretical threat. It’s here, it’s growing and it’s expensive.</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="https://www.carat.fiserv.com/en-us/resources/22nd-annual-prepaid-consumer-insights-survey?utm_source=chatgpt.com" style="font-weight: normal;">57% of consumers</a><span style="font-weight: normal;"> experienced gift card fraud in the past year</span></li>
<li><a href="https://www.carat.fiserv.com/en-us/resources/22nd-annual-prepaid-consumer-insights-survey?utm_source=chatgpt.com" style="font-weight: normal;">75% expect fraud to get worse</a><span style="font-weight: normal;"> in 2025</span><br /><span style="font-weight: normal;"></span></li>
</ul>
<p>This is more than lost revenue. Every breach damages trust in your card, in your brand and in the broader ecosystem. And as gift cards take on new roles in digital disbursements, that trust becomes non‑negotiable.</p>
<p><strong>Why this matters for merchants in 2026</strong></p>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-weight: normal;">Fraud deters purchase and redemption, hurting both sales and loyalty</span></li>
<li><span style="font-weight: normal;">Security breaches can damage reputation and increase operational costs</span></li>
<li><span style="font-weight: normal;">Buyers are increasingly cautious, with many expecting</span><a href="https://www.carat.fiserv.com/en-us/resources/22nd-annual-prepaid-consumer-insights-survey?utm_source=chatgpt.com" style="font-weight: normal;"> <span style="color: #1155cc; text-decoration: underline;">stronger protections before they’ll spend</span></a></li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Action: </strong>Prioritize protection. Choose partners like Runa who embed security from the inside out including real-time monitoring, secure code delivery&nbsp; and fraud detection built into the flow. Then, back it up with your own best practices to ensure every card is protected, trusted, and ready to redeem.</p>
