<p>We saw strong momentum in new verticals like fintech and the gig economy, where businesses are turning to gift cards for fast, flexible, and cost-effective payouts. <p>At the same time, we saw continued growth across core verticals like employee rewards, loyalty programs, and customer incentives, areas where gift cards have proven to drive engagement, boost satisfaction, and scale with ease across markets.</p>
<p>This growth was fueled by new customers like:</p>
